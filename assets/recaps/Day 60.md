# 2 days until McAllen

We had a split meeting thanks to Steel Talons letting us come by and practice at their facility.

# Team at HQ

- The team that stayed back at HQ worked more on strategy and scouting. We watched some more match videos from some events and even watched a recap of week 1 events where they interviewed 1678 on how they went about their alliance selection process. It was very insightful and we learned a lot from how one of the best teams in all of FRC makes decisions when it comes to alliance selection at events.

# Team at Katy

- We got to Katy around 6 and started practicing right away. We made big progress on the shooting and learned some more about where we need to tune our logic in our code. We started working on autons but one of the drive motor screws came out and made some loud clicking noises. We had to stop and get that addressed and then went back out for more testing but ran into another issue with not being able to stay connected to the robot. This issue is two fold as we left our POE wall plug at Texas Torque and we are pulling a lot of current that causes our power to turn off on our old white radio from like 3-4 years ago. We managed to borrow a POE wall plug from a team but by the time we got the radio and everything configured, we had to leave. 

Right now we feel pretty confident that we will do well, except we don’t have an auton routine. Tomorrow’s meeting will be the last time we can work on the robot before it hits the field at McAllen so we really need to get something working before we close up for the day.
