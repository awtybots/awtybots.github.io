# 13 days until McAllen

Since today was prom, we had our senior and junior students working pretty much all day to get the competition robot ready for the weekend. And well, we are pretty slow builders….

# Competition robot \- 90% done

- A lot of work was done to the competition robot but we did not completely finish it before the end of our meeting. We had to make a decision to either take stuff off of the competition robot and put it back on the practice robot to get it up and running again or just keep moving forward and do some work on the robot tomorrow morning at Texas Torque’s facility. Based on the last few years of not having the competition robot working and running before our first event, we chose to proceed with the comp robot because that meant that we would at least be able to run and test it 2 weeks before we have in the past. This would give us the confidence to know that when the robot shows up to McAllen, that won’t be the first time it’s been turned on, programmed and ran. It does mean that we won’t get to test the full day at Torque but hopefully it’s running by the end of the day and then we can test the full day at Pearland on Sunday.
