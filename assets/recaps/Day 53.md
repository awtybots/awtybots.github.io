# 9 days until McAllen

Today we finished up our human player tryouts and worked more on the shooter and battery cart. The cart should be finished by this weekend along with pretty much everything else.

# Shooter

- We worked on our accuracy today and we look to be pretty dialed in. We played a bit with shooting on the move but that will take some more work and tuning to get right. Unfortunately, we don’t have the space to really test autos but we hope we can try this weekend when we can move our field elements to a bigger space somewhere in the school.

# Bumpers

- The wood was cut on the CNC, glued and screwed together. The fabric will go on tomorrow and should be back in order for the competition robot. We didn’t really make any changes except fix the holes for the bumper mounts so they are more secure.

# Intake

- We are finalizing our intake tonight so we can cut it tomorrow for the meeting. We’ve been studying a lot of other teams doing a similar intake to us and the biggest difference we are seeing is that our MAXSwerve modules are really affecting the geometry of our kicker bar/plate. The driving motor is very tall and interferes with a lot of different ideas and things we want to do with the intake. That has been the biggest challenge for us to get this right.
